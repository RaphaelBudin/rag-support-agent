# Billing

Acme Cloud bills monthly based on usage. You can view invoices and update payment
details under **Settings → Billing**.

## Plans

- **Free** — 10,000 API calls/month, community support.
- **Pro** — 1,000,000 API calls/month, email support, higher rate limits.
- **Enterprise** — custom volume, SLA, and dedicated support.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and,
on annual Enterprise contracts, bank transfer (ACH / SEPA). Payment is processed
in USD. We do not accept cryptocurrency.

## Usage and overages

If you exceed your plan's included calls, additional calls are billed at the
per-call overage rate shown on the pricing page. You can set a **spend cap** under
**Settings → Billing → Limits** to have the API return `E_RATE_LIMIT` instead of
incurring overages.

## Refunds

Unused portions of annual plans are refundable on a pro-rata basis within 30 days
of a downgrade. Monthly plans are not refundable but can be cancelled anytime.
